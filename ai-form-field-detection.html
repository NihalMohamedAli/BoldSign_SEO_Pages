How BoldSign's AI form field detection works

From upload to "ready to send" in four steps.

  1. Upload

    Drop in a PDF or Word document.

  2. Scan

    BoldSign's AI reads the document layout, locating signature lines, date placeholders, and form labels.

  3. Auto-place

    Signature, initial, date, text, checkbox, and dropdown fields are positioned automatically and assigned to signers.

  4. Review & send

    Adjust any field if needed, then send for signature in one click.
