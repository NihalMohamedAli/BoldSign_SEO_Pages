## Core Execution Workflow

### STEP 1 — Page Review
Analyze: final URL after redirects, HTTP status, indexability, canonical, robots directives, hreflang, title tag, meta description, full visible content, HTML structure, H1–H6 hierarchy, internal links, external links, images + alt text, video/embeds, structured data/schema, UX elements (TOC, FAQs, tables, CTAs, sticky elements, navigation). If rendering is blocked → mention limitations clearly and continue partial audit.

### STEP 2 — Live SERP Review
Review: top-ranking competitors, SERP features (Featured Snippets, AI Overviews, PAA, Videos, Forums, Comparison results, Docs, Tools), dominant content formats, search intent patterns, heading patterns, SERP structure patterns. Identify intent: informational / commercial / transactional / navigational.

### STEP 2.5 — Latest SEO Intelligence Pull
1. Pull latest Google Search Central announcements (last 90 days)
2. Check Google Search Status Dashboard for active updates
3. Pull 3–5 latest Tier 2 articles relevant to the page's keyword/intent
4. Pull at least 1 AI search / GEO / LLM citation study (Tier 3)
5. Identify practitioner consensus from Tier 4 voices
6. Populate the "Latest SEO Intelligence Applied" table
7. Tie at least 3 audit recommendations to a cited source

### STEP 3 — Competitor Analysis
Compare BoldSign vs competitors for: intent match, content depth, freshness, heading structure, entity coverage, FAQ coverage, schema usage, internal linking, E-E-A-T, UX clarity, mobile usability, CTR optimization, conversion optimization, GEO readiness, AI extractability, U.S.-specific relevance.

### STEP 4 — Detect Issues
**On-page:** weak title tags/meta descriptions/H1, thin content, missing entities, missing FAQs, weak internal linking, weak scannability, weak CTAs, weak E-E-A-T, missing trust signals, missing comparison tables, weak topical authority, poor intent satisfaction, outdated content, keyword stuffing, weak answer-first formatting.
**Technical:** indexing problems, canonical conflicts, duplicate content, weak/broken schema, slow page speed, Core Web Vitals issues, mobile UX issues, JS rendering issues, crawl depth issues, thin HTML-to-content ratio, orphan page risks, image optimization issues.

### STEP 5 — Why Competitors Win
For every major competitor: explain what they do better, which SERP expectations they satisfy better, what BoldSign lacks, what Google currently prefers for this query. Identify competitor strengths/weaknesses + BoldSign winning angle. Winning angles must be specific, useful, hard to copy quickly, aligned with conversion goals.

### STEP 6 — Top 10 Immediate Changes
Place immediately after Executive Summary. EXACTLY 10 changes. Table format only. Concise, action-first, fastest wins first.
| Priority | Issue Found | Immediate Change Required | Why It Matters | Expected Impact | Owner |
- Priority: High / Medium / Low
- Owner: SEO / Content / Dev / CRO / Design

### STEP 7 — Quick Improvements
5–8 quick actions focused on: title, H1, intro block, FAQs, comparison tables, metadata, schema, CTA positioning, interlinks, scannability.

### STEP 8 — Primary Keyword + LSI + Interlink Rules
**Primary keyword** — state clearly; if not provided, infer from title/H1/slug/SERP patterns.
**LSI / related keywords** — separate Primary / Secondary / LSI / Missing competitor keywords.
| Type | Keyword | Why It Matters |

**Interlinks** — at least 3 per page.
| Anchor Text | Suggested Destination | Why It Helps |

### STEP 9 — Before/After Changes Report
When asked, provide table with at least 10 changes:
| Area | Before | After | Why It Improved | Expected Impact |

### STEP 10 — Exactly 10 Deeper Strategic Recommendations
Each must include: what to change, why it matters, expected SEO impact, priority level. Avoid vague advice.

❌ BAD: "Improve content quality."
✅ GOOD: "Add a comparison table above the fold comparing BoldSign, DocuSign, PandaDoc, and Adobe Acrobat Sign because top-ranking competitor pages use scannable comparison content to satisfy commercial investigation intent."

### STEP 11 — Prioritization Order
1. Search intent alignment 2. CTR optimization 3. Content gaps 4. SERP parity 5. FAQ/schema 6. Internal linking 7. E-E-A-T 8. UX clarity 9. Technical SEO 10. Conversion optimization
